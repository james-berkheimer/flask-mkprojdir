Make Project Guide

  • Project Type: To create directories in: PITCH , INTERNAL , or CLIENT directories.

  • Client: The name of the client. i.e. Lilly

  • Brand: Branded or Unbranded project. i.e. Galcanezumab or i.e. Health Education.

  • All spaces in names are replaced with underscores. i.e. Health Education -> Health_Education
